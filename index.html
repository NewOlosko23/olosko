A simple way to ship

Clear steps, quick momentum, and proof at every stage.

  1. 1
    Outcome first

    We align on the problem, audience, and a measurable goal (e.g. qualified leads, faster workflow, reduced support tickets).

  2. 2
    Lean version fast

    We ship the smallest useful version — pages, flows, and data needed to learn without delay.

    Week 1: structure + copy
    Week 2: core features
    Week 3: polish + QA
    Week 4: ship + measure
  3. 3
    Iterate with real feedback

    We adjust based on usage — simplify steps, clarify copy, and prioritize what moves the metric.
